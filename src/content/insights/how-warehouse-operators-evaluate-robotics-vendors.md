Robotics vendor evaluation is not a product-comparison exercise. It is a cross-functional decision process shaped by operations, engineering, and finance, with utilization and integration risk at the center.

## The four-stage process
1. shortlist formation
2. pilot validation
3. internal ROI modeling
4. cross-functional approval

## What operators actually stress-test
- demand volatility
- client churn
- peak mismatch
- labor substitution realism

## Cross-functional decision dynamics
### Operations
Operations teams focus on facility stability, queue behavior, and service-level protection. They test whether the system reduces recurring constraints in live conditions.

### Engineering / automation
Engineering and automation teams focus on integration quality, failure recovery, and maintainability. They evaluate implementation burden and operational control risk.

### Finance
Finance teams focus on utilization resilience, payback durability, and downside scenarios under contract and volume variability.

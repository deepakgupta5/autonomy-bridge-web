## What Happens Next
### 1. Review

Every enquiry is read in full before any response is sent. If the
engagement falls outside current capacity or scope, you will receive a
direct note rather than a redirect to a sales process.

### 2. Scoping Conversation

If there is a fit, a short working call is scheduled — 30 minutes, no
pitch. The purpose is to define the problem, not to sell a solution.

### 3. Proposal or Research Brief

Depending on enquiry type, you will receive a scoped proposal for
advisory work or a structured research brief with methodology,
deliverables, and timeline before any commitment is required.

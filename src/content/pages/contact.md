# Contact {#page-title}

## Get in Touch {#opening-statement}

Autonomy Bridge works with a specific set of clients. Use this form if you are a vendor evaluating market entry, commercial viability, ICP, or pricing in AI, robotics, or industrial automation markets; an investor conducting due diligence on a robotics or automation company or market segment; or an operator evaluating deployment economics, vendor selection, or automation program decisions.

If you are unsure whether the need is Advisory or Bespoke Research, describe the decision and Autonomy Bridge will identify the right engagement structure.

## What Happens Next {#what-happens-next}

### 1. Review

Every enquiry is read in full before any response is sent. If the engagement falls outside current capacity or scope, you will receive a direct note rather than a redirect to a sales process.

### 2. Scoping Conversation

If there is a fit, a short working call is scheduled ,  30 minutes, no pitch. The purpose is to define the problem, not to sell a solution.

### 3. Proposal or Research Brief

Depending on enquiry type, you will receive a scoped proposal for advisory work or a structured research brief with methodology, deliverables, and timeline before any commitment is required.

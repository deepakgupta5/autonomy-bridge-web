definition: "Robot density is the ratio of deployed robots to warehouse floor area or daily order volume. In warehouse automation analysis, robot density determines whether installed fleet capacity matches real operational task load. Low robot density relative to order volume indicates underdeployment; high robot density relative to order volume indicates utilization risk."
